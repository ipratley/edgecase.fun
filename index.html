What is this?

The Edge Case is a satirical comic strip documenting the real technical disasters, governance failures, and human-vs-machine absurdity of building IPQI — a systematic algorithmic trading company run by AI agents.

Every episode is based on something that actually happened. The checklist really was checked. The instruments really did not exist.
